ABOUT
TRUTHLENS

TruthLens is an open, educational news verification platform built to help readers identify misinformation and develop critical media literacy.

Our Mission

VERIFIED
NOT VIRAL.

In an era of viral misinformation, TruthLens provides a transparent, multi-source verification engine to help journalists, students, and everyday readers assess news credibility.

Our scoring algorithm analyses language patterns, source credibility markers, and cross-references content against a database of verified reports from the world's most trusted news outlets.

🔍
KEYWORD ANALYSIS
Detects credible citations vs. sensational, clickbait language patterns associated with misinformation.
🗞️
SOURCE MATCHING
Cross-references against headlines from BBC, Reuters, AP, Guardian and 5+ other tier-1 outlets.
📊
CREDIBILITY SCORING
Produces a transparent 0–100 score with a breakdown of signals contributing to the verdict.
